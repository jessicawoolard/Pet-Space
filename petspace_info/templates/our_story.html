Our Story


I have an inside/outside cat named Sponge that frequently prowls the neighborhood. I wanted a way for someone finding him to know that he was not lost, just exploring.

I have created this application that will let a pet owner create a web page for their pet with name, information, pictures and general home location. The pet’s collar could be fitted with a QR code tag, that when scanned would immediately load the pet’s web page. On the web page there is an email my owner button so the person who found your pet could contact the owner.
